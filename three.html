TRAVEL WITH US


Chikmagalur, nestled in Karnataka's Western Ghats, enchants with its lush coffee plantations and verdant hills. Visitors can trek to the picturesque peaks of Mullayanagiri, the highest in Karnataka, or explore the tranquil Hebbe Falls and Kemmangundi hills. The region's rich biodiversity awaits discovery in Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary, while ancient temples like Sringeri Sharada Peetha offer a glimpse into its cultural heritage. With its cool climate and serene ambiance, Chikmagalur beckons travelers to unwind amidst nature's splendor.
